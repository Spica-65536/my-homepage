Overview

　One of the reasons I focus on learning support using mathematics as a subject is that I once aspired to become a mathematics teacher. Mathematics is a subject that often gives rise to feelings such as “It is difficult” or “I do not understand,” and once students stumble, it can be hard for them to recover. Through my work, I hope to help learners realize that even difficult problems can be solved using their own knowledge, and to support them in overcoming their anxiety toward mathematics.
　Outside the laboratory, I work as a tutor at a cram school. I enjoy teaching and supporting others, and I also place strong emphasis on mentoring junior students in the laboratory.
